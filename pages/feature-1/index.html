Valorant is free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Windows. Teased uncer the codename Project A in October 2019, the game beegan a closed data beta period with limited access on April 7, 2020 followed by a release on June 2, 2020.

Features

Unique Agents

Experience the thrill of quick matches with Swiftplay mode, designed for fast-paced action.

Tactical Gameplay

Test your skills and rise through the ranks in Competitive mode, where every match matters.

Ranked Competitive Mode

Play casually in Unrated mode, perfect for warming up or enjoying the game without pressure.
