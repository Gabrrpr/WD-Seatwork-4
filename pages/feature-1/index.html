Valorant is free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Windows. Teased uncer the codename Project A in October 2019, the game beegan a closed data beta period with limited access on April 7, 2020 followed by a release on June 2, 2020.

Features

Unique Agents

Each agent you play has unique abilities and plays a specific role in the game. There are for main roles: Duelists, Initiators, Sentinels, and Controllers Each Role and agent brings different strategies and skills to the team, creating a diverse and dynnamic gameplay experience.

Tactical Gameplay

In Valorant, tactical gameplay is a fundamental aspect that sets it apart from many other first-person shooters. It emphasizes strategy, team coordination, and the effective use of each agent's abilities.

Ranked Competitive Mode

Ranked Competitive Mode in Valorant, often reffered to simply as "Ranked", is strctured environment where players can test their skills and strive to improve their rank by competing against others of similar skill levels.
